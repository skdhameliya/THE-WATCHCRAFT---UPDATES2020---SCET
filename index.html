    The Watch Craft

    About The Event

    • Non-Technical Event
    • Questions are based on Popular Movies & TV-Series such as Dialogues, Characters, Intro, Music, Casts, etc.
    • Three fastest performers in Round 3 will be declared as a Winners.
    • The users will be provided with a web-interface to complete the event and various techniques like head-to-head, fastest finger, etc can be used in each round.

    Instructions

    Participation Mode : Single

    Number of Rounds : 3

    Round-1 :

    This round wil be open for all participants from 08:30 AM to 11:00 AM. You can login at any time during this time period only and answer the quiz question as mention. You will have 20 questions and 3 minutes to complete the quiz once you start (No time limit for each question). More score and least time will be considered first. 50% participants will go for next round.

    Only one chance is there to complete the quiz.

    You can not go back once the quiz is started.

    Do not refresh the quiz page, if you will refresh it then new question will be displayed and your score will be counted from 0 but your time will not be resetted.

    You have successfully completed round-1.

    Round-2 :

    Please join google meet for instructions. click here to join

    This round wil be open for all participants from 12:45 PM to 03:00 PM. You can login during this time and answer the quiz question as mention. You will be given pictures from Famous Webseries. You will have 6 mintues to answer as much questions as you can. 30% participants will go for next round.

    Round-3 :

    This round wil be open for all participants from 04:00 PM to 05:00 PM. You can login during this time and answer the quiz question as mention. You will be given short clips from Famous Webseries. You will have 15 questions and 5 minutes to answer all. Top 3 perfomers based on max. score and min. time will be declared as winners.
